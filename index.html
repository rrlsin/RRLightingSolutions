Who We Are

A Legacy of Precision & Light

With over two and a half decades of experience, RR Lighting Solutions (RRLS) has established itself as India's foremost authority in professional floodlighting consultancy. Our body of work spans some of the most prestigious sporting venues, industrial complexes, ports, and airports across the nation and abroad.

We do not merely install lighting systems — we architect luminous environments engineered for performance, longevity, and efficiency. Our singular expertise encompasses the full project lifecycle, from initial feasibility assessment to commissioning, and continues through the operational life of every system we deliver.

At RRLS, we offer a comprehensive one-stop solution for your entire turnkey electrical infrastructure — eliminating coordination complexity and ensuring seamless project delivery under one trusted roof.

What We Do

Our Comprehensive Service Portfolio

From the procurement of a single luminaire to the delivery of an entire stadium lighting ecosystem — RRLS provides an unmatched breadth of specialist services, each executed with the same rigour and precision that has defined our practice since inception.

01
New Installations
Design, supply, and installation of cutting-edge MH and LED floodlighting systems for sports facilities, industrial spaces, airports, and ports — executed to the highest engineering standards, both domestically and internationally.
MH & LED Systems
02
LED Retrofits & Overhaul
A complete conversion service for legacy Metal Halide systems to energy-efficient, broadcast-grade LED technology. We assess, design, procure, and execute with zero-compromise on luminous performance.
Energy Efficiency
03
Aiming & Re-Aiming
Precision photometric aiming and re-aiming services to eliminate glare, correct spill light, and ensure uniformity ratios meet international broadcast and sporting standards, including BCCI, ICC, FIFA, and more.
Photometric Precision
04
Servicing & AMC
Structured Annual Maintenance Contracts and responsive servicing to guarantee the sustained performance of your lighting infrastructure, minimising unplanned downtime and extending asset life.
Long-Term Partnership
05
Musco MH System Buy-Back
We facilitate the professional dismantling, valuation, and buy-back of existing Musco Metal Halide systems across India and internationally — enabling a clean transition to modern, efficient alternatives.
India & International

Consultancy & Design
Independent expert consultancy spanning lighting design, electrical infrastructure planning, regulatory compliance, and project management — delivering unbiased, client-centric guidance at every stage.
