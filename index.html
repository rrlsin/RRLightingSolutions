Who We Are

A Legacy of
Precision & Light

With over two and a half decades of experience, RR Lighting Solutions (RRLS) has established itself as India's foremost authority in professional floodlighting consultancy. Our body of work spans some of the most prestigious sporting venues, industrial complexes, ports, and airports across the nation and abroad.

We do not merely install lighting systems — we architect luminous environments engineered for performance, longevity, and efficiency. Our expertise encompasses the full project lifecycle, from initial feasibility assessment through to commissioning, and continues through the operational life of every system we deliver.

At RRLS, we offer a comprehensive one-stop solution for your entire turnkey electrical infrastructure — eliminating coordination complexity and ensuring seamless project delivery under one trusted roof.
